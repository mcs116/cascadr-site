Questions & Answers

What kind of content can I paste in?

Any long-form text: blog posts, articles, podcast transcripts, video scripts, newsletter drafts, meeting notes, whitepapers — anything over ~200 words. The more context Cascadr has, the better the outputs.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a general chatbot. Cascadr is a specialized tool built specifically for content repurposing. Every output is optimized for the target platform's format, character limits, culture, and algorithm preferences. You don't need to write prompts or know what works on each platform — Cascadr does.

Can I customize the output tone?

Yes. You can set brand voice profiles (professional, casual, witty, authoritative, etc.) and Cascadr will match your tone across all platforms while adapting to each platform's norms.

What's a "repurpose"?

One repurpose = one piece of source content transformed into outputs for your selected platforms. If you paste a blog post and select Twitter + LinkedIn + Email, that's one repurpose producing three outputs.

Do I own the output content?

Yes, 100%. Everything Cascadr generates is yours to use, publish, modify, and monetize however you want.
